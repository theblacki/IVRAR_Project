Project Introduction In the context of the lecture Interaction in Augmented Reality and Virtual Reality (IARVR or IVRAR), I created a VR application tasking the user to overcome a parkour. The idea is that the XR environment provides a completely new context of perception and interaction, with the rather obvious problem of having to design applications around these exact new systems and tools. More specific, the task is to design methods of interaction and locomotion within a VR environment and implement them in a way that makes both testable.
